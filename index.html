About Hooks Lawn Care

Built on Hard Work & Honest Service

Hooks Lawn Care was founded right here in Charlotte with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a lawn they're proud of, without the hassle or the runaround. Over the past 12 years, we've become one of the most trusted names in lawn care across Charlotte, Ballantyne, Mint Hill, and beyond.

We're fully licensed, insured, and committed to showing up on time, doing the job right, and treating your property like it's our own. No shortcuts, no upsells you don't need — just clean, professional results every single visit.
